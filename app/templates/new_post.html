Guidelines for posting
  • Ask questions that you will know when an answer is satisfactory, e.g. because the answerer convinced you of their credential through a timestamp, or because the correctness of the answer can be verified by yourself, once enough proof is presented
  • Be specific, give criteria on who you want to hear from if possible
  • Use hashtags so the relevant people can find your post quicker, e.g. if your question is about New York, put #NewYork somewhere in the Details section for it to be recognized as a hashtag
  • Spread the word and share your post using social media and other online communities, but please follow their rules and don't spam. Generally, a good way is to ask the same question in the community and offer to pay a reward using the link to your post here
  • Don't put any personal information in the post
  • Enable Social Media mode to offer even more incentive for people to spread the word by guaranteeing a referral reward
Payments and rewards
  • Payment is not required when a post is created
  • If someone takes an interest in your post and makes an engagement request, the buyer's account balance will be checked before the engagement can be entered into, and a top-up will be required if there is not enough funds to make a payment for the reward offered
  • If both parties rate the engagement as successful(+), a reward will be distributed according to the reward distribution rules
  • If the buyer rates the engagement as unsuccessful(-), no reward will be distributed and the reserved amount will be released back to the buyer's account
  • The track record of each user is available for all users to see on the user profile page, it is also used model a user's trustworthiness on the platform which limits the reward value of posts the user is allowed to participate in
