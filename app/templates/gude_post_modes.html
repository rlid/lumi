Hey, we have a new feature for you!

Standard Mode
  • Like your traditional online forum where your post is listed on our front page and people discover posts by browsing the platform
  • Each referrer can earn up to 10% of the post reward, total referral reward for all referrers is caped at 20% of post reward
  • The answerer gets the remaining reward less our 10% platform fee (i.e. 90% if no referrer to 70% minimum)
  • Referral reward is optional and shared at the discretion of the user who entered the engagement with the OP
Social Media (a.k.a. Stealth) Mode
  • The post lives on Social Media networks (Twitter, Facebook, Reddit or other online communities) and travels the world with the OP or referrers sharing it with their network of friends to spread the word
  • The post is not directly accessible on our platform and can only be accessed through links shared by the OP or the referrers
  • Each referrer can earn up to 20%(*) of the post reward, total referral reward for all referrers is caped at 40%(*) of post reward
  • Final answerer gets the remaining reward less our 10% platform fee (i.e. 90% if no referrer to 70% minimum)
  • Referral rewards are compulsory and guaranteed to be paid out!
(*) In Power User Mode (coming soon), the allocation of reward can be further customised where the OP can set an almost arbitrary referral budget for the referrers to claim, and each referrer can decide for themselves how much to claim from pot, depending on how confident he is at directly finding someone to engage with OP and how much incentive he wants to offer to the subsequent referrers.
