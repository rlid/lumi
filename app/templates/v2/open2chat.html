How it works

  1. create your own AMA "tip jar"
  2. others click the link to send you a message and a "tip"
  3. you claim the tip by responding to the message

And that's it, just a simple tip jar with slightly more benefit for the tippers. Now the question is, will it get you more tips in the jar?

Who is it for

If you are like most of us, you are not using social media for business reasons, and you probably don't expect to be tipped for the stuff you post and share.

But from time to time, there are people who read what you posted by chance, and you just happen to be their ideal person (and the only one they could find) to chat about something interesting related to your content.

Most will probably not message you as it is pretty awkward to do, and you might not appreciate random questions from strangers.

This link should make the experience better for both of you - you get to help strangers and get tipped, and they get to reach you in a less awkward way and show their appreciation for your time and kindness.

Who this is NOT for: if you are looking to grow a following and drive up engagements on social media, you probably don't want a roadblock like this between you and your audience, but we won't stop you!
