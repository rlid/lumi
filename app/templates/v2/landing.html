FAQ

How long does it take to find a match?

We currently aim to find a relevant match for your topic within 24 hours. We are constantly expanding the network and improving our algorithm to make it quicker. You may also consider offering a larger reward to speed it up.

When is payment taken?

You'll be asked to top up your account before you can engage with your match. The funds will not leave your account unless you rate the engagement successful. You can get a full refund when you cancel the search.

What information about me is shared with my match?

Just the details of the search you provided. In particular, your email address is not shared with your match. You and your match will remain mutually anonymous to each other.

Can I use a different channel to communicate with my match?

Yes if both of you are comfortable and agree to use something else. Just remember to come back and rate your engagement.

How can I be sure my match is who they claim to be?

Your match's credential will be checked by our contributors and if applicable, by other users who engaged with your match on relevant searches in the past. We can also provide any proof your match is willing to share at your request.
